The regulatory surface I know firsthand

Where AI quietly creates liability.

These aren't abstractions I read about. They're the constraints I've operated under for twenty years — which is exactly why I can see where AI runs into them.

  • Fair housing — protected-class and steering language slipping into AI-generated marketing and listing copy.
  • USPAP independence — keeping AI out of value influence and the appraisal workfile intact.
  • RSO / AB 1482 — rent-setting logic and tenant communications screened against rent regulation.
  • Data licensing — MLS and third-party use terms respected when data feeds a model.
  • Confidentiality & E&O — client and borrower data handling that holds up.
  • Shadow AI — the tools already in use through staff, with no policy and no approval path.
One caveat, stated plainly: this is governance and advisory work, not legal advice. I help you build AI use that respects fair housing, USPAP, RSO/AB 1482, and your data-licensing terms — and I'll tell you clearly when something genuinely needs your counsel.
